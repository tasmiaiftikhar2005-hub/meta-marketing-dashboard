The Course Curriculum

6 complete modules. Real strategies. Professional-grade content.

📌 Module 1: Foundations

The digital marketing revolution, why Meta dominates e-commerce, psychology of social shopping, essential assets setup, and campaign architecture. Your complete starting point.

⚙️ Module 2: Technical Tracking

Meta Pixel & Dataset setup, Shopify-Meta integration, solving iOS 14 with CAPI, domain verification, WordPress tracking, and verification protocols for 99% data accuracy.

🎬 Module 3: Creative Strategy

The 3-part video framework, 4 proven hook architectures, winning ad formats (UGC, Before/After, Lifestyle), AI-powered scripting with ChatGPT, and creative performance metrics.

📊 Module 4: Testing & Scaling

The ABO testing framework, the 24/48/72-hour data analysis rule, vertical & horizontal scaling strategies, Post ID method, Advantage+ Catalog Ads, and automated scaling rules.

🔁 Module 5: Retargeting & Retention

Converting warm audiences, the 10-15% retargeting budget rule, custom audience building, creative strategy for retargeting, Lookalike Audiences, and maximizing Customer Lifetime Value.

💼 Module 6: Business & Clients

Campaign monitoring, professional client reporting, client acquisition roadmap from zero, micro-niche specialization, self-branding strategy, and leveraging freelance platforms.
